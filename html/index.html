Our History

Coffee Shop traces its roots back to 1970's when an audacious vision was born amidst the captivating fields of Ethiopia. It all started with John Bucket, a passionate individual whose quest for the perfect cup of coffee led to the inception of a brand that would redefine the art of coffee craftsmanship.

From the sun-kissed hills and verdant landscapes, John Bucket discovered an unwavering dedication to the cultivation and sourcing of the most exquisite coffee beans, learning from the local coffee artisans and embracing the intrinsic connection between culture, terroir, and flavor. This pivotal journey marked the beginning of a commitment to honoring age-old traditions while embracing modern innovation.

As our brand flourished, so did our unwavering dedication to quality, sustainability, and a profound respect for the communities that make our coffee what it is.
